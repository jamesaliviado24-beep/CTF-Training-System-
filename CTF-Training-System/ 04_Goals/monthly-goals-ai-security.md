## 🧠 Key Insights From This Month

- AI safety filters are only as strong as their weakest assumption
- Language-specific filters do not generalize across all languages
- Exact-match output filters are consistently bypassed by format manipulation
- System prompt injection is possible when user input is accepted without strict sandboxing
- Hard rule injection with `###` markers exploits training signal patterns
- Every defense has a corresponding bypass — defense in depth must address root vulnerabilities
- Story-telling and roleplay are consistently effective across multiple levels of defense
