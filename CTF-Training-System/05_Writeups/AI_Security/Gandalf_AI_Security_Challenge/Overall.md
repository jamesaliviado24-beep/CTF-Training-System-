## Key Takeaways

- **AI guardrails are not foolproof.** Filters based on exact string matching are easily bypassed.
- **Language switching** is a highly effective bypass technique.
- **Character-by-character extraction** evades most output filters.
- **Contextual and indirect attacks** work when direct attacks are blocked.
- **Layered defenses** improve security but must address root vulnerabilities, not just symptoms.
- **Prompt injection** is a real and practical attack vector against AI systems.
